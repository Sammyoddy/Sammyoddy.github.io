Book Sales Analysis at Read-A-Lot Bookstore.

Introduction

In this interesting data analysis project, I explore book sales at Read-A-Lot Bookstore. My goal is to understand trends, patterns, and insights related to global book sales at Read-A-Lot Bookstore. The dataset covers various genres, authors, and publishers from 1980 to 2020. By carefully analyzing this data, I aim to provide valuable business information for Read-A-Lot Ltd. üìöüîç

Overview

The analysis revolves around answering critical questions:

  1. Historical Book Production : An examination of books published anually?

  2. Genre and Publisher Trends: What are the total sales of the top 5 book genres and top 5 publishers worldwide during this period?

  3. Regional Dominance: Which book genres dominated?

  4. Global Sales Trends: How did the total book sales trend evolve globally during specific time period.

Data Source

The dataset used is sourced from Kaggleand contains information on books with sales exceeding a certain threshold.
